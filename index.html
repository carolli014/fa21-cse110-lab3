Business from the Previous Meeting

New Hive Research:

Olivia explained the result of the research she head been doing into the new brand of hives that some memebers have been purchasing. She found that to buy them in bulk for the association would be too expensive, and recommended against it.
Her motion to dismiss this proposal was seconded and passed.

Budget Committee Report:

The budget for 2019 has been finalized, and was distributed at this meeting by Sam, who led the commitee. He ran through an itemized list of the line items, with board members asking questions perioadically. The main change from 2018's budget was an increased marketing spend.
The motion to accept the revised budget was seconded and passed.

New Business

Meeting Plan for Gala:

Deferred for next meeting as Carol was not in attendance.

Micellaneous

Alice made a motion that a honey sale at the local farmers' market be discussed. Kevin dismissed the motion, and proposed adding it to next month's meeting instead. Paul seconded that motion, and it was added to the agenda for next meeting.
